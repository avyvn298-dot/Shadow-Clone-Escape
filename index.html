Shadow Clone Escape

Survive as long as possible — a new solvable maze each run.

Best: —

Tutorial

  • Move with WASD / arrow keys or on-screen controls (mobile).
  • Avoid clones — they replay your past path.
  • Collect power-ups (Speed/Cloak/Shockwave) to survive longer.
  • Try to set a top time — leaderboard saves best runs locally.

    Controls

    Keyboard: WASD / Arrow keys. Mobile: on-screen D-Pad + Power button.

    Power-ups

    • Speed — movement boost for a short time.
    • Cloak — clones ignore you briefly.
    • Shockwave — pushes nearby clones back along their path.

    Tips

    • Plan your path — clones replay your exact steps.
    • Use corridors to funnel clones past you.
    • Save power-ups for tight situations.
